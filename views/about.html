Welcome

Hedonimetry: the study of happiness as a measurable economic asset. It is gauged by a device called a Hedonimeter, as Francis Ysidro Edgeworth said to describe, "an ideally perfect instrument, a psychophysical machine, continually registering the height of pleasure experienced by an individual."

moodX uses deep-rich social media to track keywords in an user's post to provide accurate data on moods. The main priciple data coming from Twitter, the hedonimeter can coorelate the data from tweets and statuses to measure emotional levels on graphs. Our charts provide data from the start of Twitter till 26 months from the current date along with powerful search engines. The New York Times feature headline news from as early as 1851. The DuckDuckGo browser gives fast responses and doesn't track or collect information to value your privacy.
